What is FthPyces?

Find Those Pyces is a game where players search for and collect Pyces (robotic beings with special abilities) in a unique universe full of challenges. Discover more about them!

How do you play?

Your mission is to find as many Pyces as you can in Techspawn. There are all kinds and varying difficulties... so be ready!

What can I find here?

Here, you'll mostly find Pyces, obbies, new characters, and you might even find me! The great KirByte_Bi, Pyces designer and leader of this place.
