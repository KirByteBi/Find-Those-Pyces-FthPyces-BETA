Pyces Difficulty

PERMANENT / NON-EXCLUSIVE

Basic (Level 0)

Standard Pyce with no modifications. No difficulty.

Easy (Level 1)

Visible and predictable Pyces. Perfect for beginners.

Medium (Level 2)

Diverse Pyces with simple but varied patterns.

Challenging (Level 3)

Intermediate challenge between Medium and Hard.

Hard (Level 4)

High difficulty but no extreme mechanics.

Advanced (Level 5)

Pyces with advanced scripts or methods.

Expert (Level 6)

Requires advanced tricks or external knowledge (e.g., binary).

Ultra (Level 7)

Maximum difficulty. Grants the "Ultra Catch" badge.
