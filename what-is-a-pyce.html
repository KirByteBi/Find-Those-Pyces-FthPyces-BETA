What is a Pyce?

Pyces are small robotic creatures with bluish metallic bodies. Key features:

  • Hand: A computer mouse.
  • Foot: A broken keyboard key.
  • True Form: They once had a chip that powered then into "true form", a blue Pyce that was captable of using the energy for his own... After that, none got that power.
  • Abilities: They have control over the portals, which they call Portalogy, and they study it to create portals to other Techspawn satellites.
